# Course Reflection: Growth and Application

**Due:** March 11, 2026
**Points:** 20

---

## Overview

As we conclude this course, take time to reflect on your learning journey and consider how you will apply statistical knowledge in your professional practice. This reflection is an opportunity to consolidate your learning and articulate your growth.

---

## Reflection Prompts

In a thoughtful reflection (500-750 words), address the following questions:

### 1. Learning Growth (6 points)

- How has your understanding of statistics changed since the beginning of this course?
- What concepts were most challenging for you, and how did you overcome those challenges?
- What was your most significant "aha moment" during the course?

### 2. Professional Application (6 points)

- How do you plan to apply statistical knowledge in your current or future role in higher education?
- What specific analyses or techniques do you anticipate using most frequently?
- How has this course changed how you consume statistical information in research or reports?

### 3. Continued Learning (4 points)

- What areas of statistics would you like to explore further?
- What resources or strategies will you use to continue developing your statistical skills?
- How will you stay current with statistical methods in your field?

### 4. Course Feedback (4 points)

- What aspects of the course were most helpful for your learning?
- What suggestions do you have for improving the course for future students?
- What advice would you give to someone just starting this course?

---

## Submission Requirements

- **Format:** Word document, PDF, or text entry
- **Length:** 500-750 words
- **Style:** First-person reflective writing is appropriate

This reflection is personal and there are no "wrong" answers. The goal is thoughtful engagement with the prompts and honest assessment of your learning experience. Your feedback will help improve future iterations of this course.
