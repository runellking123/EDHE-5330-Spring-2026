## Quiz Overview

This quiz assesses your understanding of correlation coefficients, scatterplot interpretation, and simple linear regression concepts.

---

## Topics Covered

- Calculating and interpreting correlation coefficients (r)
- Distinguishing between positive and negative correlations
- Understanding correlation vs. causation
- Simple linear regression equation components (Y = a + bX)
- Interpreting slope and intercept
- R-squared and explained variance
- Identifying confounding variables
