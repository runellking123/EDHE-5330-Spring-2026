## Learning Objectives

By the end of Week 1, you will be able to:

- Define statistics and distinguish between descriptive and inferential statistics
- Identify and classify variables according to level of measurement
- Explain the role of statistics in evidence-based educational decision-making
- Evaluate examples of statistical use in educational research and practice
