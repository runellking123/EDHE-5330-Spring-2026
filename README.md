## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. Define and apply descriptive and inferential statistical methods
2. Calculate and interpret measures of central tendency and variability
3. Understand probability concepts and the normal distribution
4. Evaluate sampling methods and identify potential biases
5. Calculate and interpret correlation coefficients
6. Conduct and interpret hypothesis tests
7. Perform t-tests and ANOVA for group comparisons
8. Communicate statistical findings effectively
