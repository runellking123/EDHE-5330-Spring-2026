## Course Description

This course introduces graduate students to fundamental statistical concepts and their application to higher education research and practice. Students will develop skills in data analysis, interpretation, and communication of statistical findings.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

1. Apply descriptive and inferential statistical methods to educational data
2. Interpret statistical results in meaningful ways for educational contexts
3. Evaluate the appropriateness of statistical methods in research
4. Communicate statistical findings to diverse audiences
5. Use technology (Excel) to conduct basic statistical analyses
6. Critically consume statistical information in educational research and policy
